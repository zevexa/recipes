Mama's Mac and Cheese

Let me tell you 'bout Mama

When I was young, I didn't get allowance like other kids; I got mac and cheese

I'd eat it day and night, and after years of eating mac and cheese day in and day out, I eventually became a bowl of mac and cheese. The image above is me at a family reunion.

I eventually had to become human again before I spoiled and had to be thrown away, but I'll remember those days fondly, and often make Mama's recipe for my family, though I can't eat any of it due to the risk involved.
