Mama's Mac and Cheese

Let me tell you 'bout Mama

When I was young, I didn't get allowance like other kids; I got mac and cheese

I'd eat it day and night, and after years of eating mac and cheese day in and day out, I eventually became a bowl of mac and cheese. The image above is me at a family reunion.

I eventually had to become human again before I spoiled and had to be thrown away, but I'll remember those days fondly and often make Mama's recipe for my family, though I can't eat any of it due to the risk involved.

Proceed with caution

Ingredients

  • A box of your favorite noodle. Don't use homemade, as I can only measure in box
  • 40 oz of cheese. Use cheeses - be creative!
  • 1 cup Mother's milk
  • Some flour
  • A small - medium box of heavy cream
  • 2-8 cloves of garlic
  • Cayenne pepper - I accidentally spilled 1/3 of the container in my last batch, and it went well! Follow your heart!
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Tears

Just like Mama Made It

  1. Boil some water and put your noods in
  2. Make a rue by using the butter I forgot to put into the ingredients list, and the flour I included
  3. Add some of the cheeses you shredded or blended (depending on the cheeses you chose) to the rue
  4. Once your noods are just barely al dente, drain, rinse with cool water, and set aside
  5. When your cheese-rue hits a specific consistency, pour it into your mac, which you put in a large bowl, I hope
  6. Oh yeah, add spices to the rue before you pour
  7. Layer your remaining cheese and noodles like this: noodles, cheese, noodles, cheese, so on, so forth
  8. Put in your preheated oven set to 350 for 1-2 hours. It's bests to use instinct rather than a timer. Or maybe I should have timed it. One of those, you got this!
  9. If you plan to eat this day in and day out, be sure to take breaks so you don't turn into a bowl of mac and cheese
  10. ENJOY!
